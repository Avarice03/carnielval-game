Rock Paper Scissors

How to Play:

  1. Choose a weapon by clicking a button in the player section: Rock, Paper, or Scissors
  2. Enter the amount of Niel coins you want to wager in the wager section
  3. Click the play button after locking your wager
    • Rock wins over scissors
    • Scissors wins over Paper
    • Paper wins over rock
    • Same weapons will result in a draw
  4. Win the game by raising 10000 Niels
  5. Enjoy! 😄

Color Game

How to Play:

  1. Choose a color by clicking a button in the player section: Blue, Red, Green, Yellow, White, or Pink
  2. Enter the amount of Niel coins you want to wager in the wager section
  3. Click the play button to pull the string after locking your wager
    • There are three dice with each sides having six different colors
    • You will win the amount of coins you wagered with every color you selected matching the color generated by each dice
    • You can win up to 1x, 2x, or 3x of your wager
  4. Win the game by raising 10000 Niels
  5. Enjoy! 😄
